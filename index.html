(latest)build.

Although not a complete build, the update to my personal computer was substantial enough to list as it consisited of an entirely new GPU, Motherboard, RAM and 4tb of SATA SSD/NVMe storage. The performance uplift I saw in games were massive, mostly due to the GPU upgrade, but the replacement of my old HDD's helped with responsiveness and 1% lows. The motherboard upgrade was mostly so I could switch from a Daisy Chain RAM topology to a
T-topology to give me more leeway when increasing my RAM timings.
